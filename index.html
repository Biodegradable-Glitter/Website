Who are we?

Run by the infamous Goofy Goobers, Biodegradable Glitter is an independent and completely biased film review site. Many would even go so far as to say we are the interweb's besterest such site.

Unlike our trusted competitors (pictured left), we have no idea what we're doing here. We just do the minimum amount of work to not get cancelled and then increase fluffiness by 117%.


Why are we called 'Biodegradable Glitter'?

You see, in the olden days there was no Netflix or Disney+, so people went to the theater and watched funny people pretend to do funny things, and everybody thought this was funny. But, if the funny people were not funny and were just people, the people watching would throw rotten red vegetables (or fruit, depending on who you ask) at the not-so-funny people.

In 1998, some weirdo movie nerd made a website and named it after the red fruit that was thrown at bad performers. In 2018, some weirdo engineering nerd had a package stolen from his porch. This particular weirdo made a glitter bomb to get revenge on porch pirates and, for five years, has been putting increasingly complicated such bombs in increasingly random places, and getting sweet, sweet revenge with Biodegradable Glitter.

In 2024, the Goofy Goobers were at school and were told to make a discursive comparison between Spider-Man into the Spider-Verse and Princess Mononoke. They decided to make a funny film review website and named it after the colourful Biodegradable Glitter used by that weird engineering nerd to get revenge on porch pirates because of the other film review site that was named after that rotten red fruit.
